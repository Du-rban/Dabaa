About Kenya Police SACCO

Kenya Police SACCO is a member-based savings and credit cooperative dedicated to providing financial services exclusively to Kenya Police Service officers. We believe in the power of collective savings and mutual support within our police community.

With years of experience in financial services, we've helped thousands of police officers achieve their financial goals through secure savings, affordable loans, and comprehensive financial products.
